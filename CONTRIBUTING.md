# Contributing

Contributions are welcome. Two lightweight requirements before a change can be
merged: **sign off your commits** (DCO) and **agree to the CLA**.

## License

This project is released under the license in [`LICENSE`](LICENSE). By
contributing, your contribution is provided under that same license (and see the
CLA below regarding future re-licensing).

## 1. Sign off your commits (DCO)

Every commit must carry a `Signed-off-by` line, which you add automatically with:

```bash
git commit -s
```

This line certifies the **Developer Certificate of Origin** (below): that you
wrote the change, or otherwise have the right to submit it under this project's
license. Use your real name and a reachable email.

## 2. Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

By opening a pull request you also agree to the [Contributor License
Agreement](CLA.md). In short: **you keep the copyright to your work**, but you
grant the maintainer the right to license — and re-license — your contribution,
including under different or commercial terms. This is what lets the project stay
flexible about its licensing later. Read [`CLA.md`](CLA.md) for the full terms.
